# HomeFeast Requirements

This document contains the detailed guide and project requirements for the HomeFeast – Homemade Tiffin & Food Service Platform analysis.

## Context
HomeFeast is a web-based platform that connects users (students, working professionals, elderly people) with verified home cooks and tiffin service providers offering fresh, hygienic, home-style meals on a daily, weekly, or monthly subscription basis. The platform digitizes ordering, subscription management, and service coordination.

## Problem Statement
Many people living away from home struggle to find healthy, affordable, and hygienic homemade food. Current food delivery apps focus mainly on restaurants, leading to:

- High cost of meals
- Unhealthy or repetitive food
- No long-term meal subscription options
- Limited visibility of local home cooks
- Manual coordination via calls or WhatsApp

## Primary Objectives
- Digitize homemade tiffin and food service management
- Enable easy discovery of local home cooks
- Provide flexible meal subscriptions
- Improve food access for students and professionals

## Secondary Objectives
- Support small home-based food businesses
- Improve transparency in menus and pricing
- Enable scalable expansion across cities
